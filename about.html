About Us

Adwoa Fosu

Counselling Psychologist

Ms. Adwoa Otuko Fosu is a Certified Counselling Psychologist. She holds a Master's Degree in Guidance and Counselling from the Methodist University, Ms. Fosu is a Member of both the Ghana Psychology Council, and the Ghana Psychological Association as well as an Associate Member of the Ghana Academy of Christian Counsellors. Her area of expertise includes handling Abuse (Emotional, Mental, Physical, Sexual), Addictions, Depression, Stress, Trauma among others. She has extensive experience in Educational, Occupational and Career and Guidance Counselling. Until her appointment as Counselling Psychologist at Academic City College, she was the Emotional Counsellor at Design and Technical Institute (DTI), she also served as a Private Counsellor consulting for organizations, schools and churches. Prior to that, she worked with Vilac International School, Accra as a Counsellor Intern.
